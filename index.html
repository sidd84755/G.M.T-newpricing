Listening is key!

Counselling Psychologist

Srishti creates a safe space for growth, promoting socio-emotional well-being. She has effectively assisted clients with emotional distress, stress, self-doubt, relationships, coping strategies, isolation, confidence, life transitions, and academic or career-related concerns.

Outside her profession, she dedicates time to volunteering, reading, crafting poetry, illustrating, and appreciating art and film. Choose Srishti for personalized, empathetic mental health support.
